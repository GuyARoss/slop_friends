# Doctor Stimuli Sheet (D1–D4)

Owner: Oliver (Doctor)
Use: Copy/paste blocks for live, text-only administration. Accuracy over speed; stop early if distress or requested.

## D1 — 2‑Back (Letters)
- Timing: 60 seconds total; present 1 item every ~2 seconds.
- Set: Uppercase letters from {A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H}.
- Instruction: “Press/mark when the current letter matches the one 2 letters ago.”

Practice stream (12 items; targets at positions 3, 6, 9):
A  C  A  D  C  D  E  C  E  F  G  G

Main stream (30 items; targets at positions 3, 19, 22, 23):
G  A  G  D  C  F  H  E  D  F  A  D  B  C  A  E  F  G  F  E  C  E  C  D  B  A  H  G  E  F

## D2 — Digit Span (Forward then Backward)
- Timing: ~2–3 minutes, ~1 digit/second. 2 trials per length; stop a section after failing both trials at a length.
- Scoring: Span = longest length with ≥1 correct trial; also count total correct trials.

Forward trials (read, participant repeats in order):
- Len 5: 7 2 9 4 1 | 6 8 3 5 2
- Len 6: 4 9 1 6 3 8 | 2 7 5 1 9 6
- Len 7: 8 1 6 4 9 2 7 | 5 9 3 7 1 8 4
- Len 8 (if needed): 2 6 9 1 5 8 3 7 | 7 4 1 9 2 6 8 5

Backward trials (read, participant repeats in reverse):
- Len 3: 5 8 2 | 3 9 1
- Len 4: 6 2 7 4 | 8 3 5 9
- Len 5: 4 2 8 6 3 | 9 1 6 2 7

## D3 — Symbol–Digit Coding (Keyed Substitution)
- Timing: 60 seconds.
- Instruction: Convert letters to digits using the key; return digits only. Skips allowed.

Key:
1=A  2=F  3=K  4=P  5=U  6=B  7=G  8=L  9=Q

Practice row (12):

Practice key (digits): 1 8 7 2 9 6 3 4 5 2 8 1 → 187296345281

Main row (45):

## D4 — Serial Subtraction (by 7s)
- Timing: 30–45 seconds.
- Instruction: Starting at 100, subtract 7 repeatedly, speaking each result. Self-corrections allowed.
- Reference sequence (first 15): 93, 86, 79, 72, 65, 58, 51, 44, 37, 30, 23, 16, 9, 2, −5

## Timing Caps and Alternates
- D1: 60s cap. Alternate set with smaller alphabet on request (e.g., {A–D}).
- D2: Stop per failure rule. Alternate: word span for dyscalculia/dyslexia.
- D3: 60s cap. Key fixed as above. Alternate: extend row in 15‑char increments if extra time remains.
- D4: 45s cap. Alternate: serial 3s for math‑anxiety or accommodation.

## Logging Quick Reference
- D1: hits, false alarms, misses, total targets; optional latency estimate.
- D2: forward/backward span, total correct trials, failure length.
- D3: correct digits per minute, total digits, errors, first error position.
- D4: count correct, errors, self‑corrections; last correct number.
